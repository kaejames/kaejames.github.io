Major Political Events of 2022

Boris Johnson stepped down as Prime Minister on September 5th 2022 after MP Lizz Truss won the conservative leadership poll and was sworn as the new Prime Minister on the 6th

On September 23rd 2022, PM Liz Truss and Chancellor of the Exchequer Kwasi Kwarteng announced economic policies that were reffered to as a “mini budget”. The stock market didn’t like the new policies and the sterling pound dropped to it’s lowest against the dollar since the 1980’s. This caused the Conservative party to drop in polls, to it’s lowest since the late 90’s ahead on 1997 Labor landslide victory.
