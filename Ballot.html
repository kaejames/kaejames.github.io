Congressional Polling & Women Voters

10/ 02/ 22


Polling average of the 2022 midterms elections using Echelon Insights, Fox News Poll, NYT/Siena, Cygnal,Selzer&Co and Monmouth University Poll. These are high quality pollsters with a great record when it comes to polls conducted the past two years especially Echelon, Fox and Monmouth for their Virginia 2021 Gubernatorial election.


The Republicans led the polling average from late January to early July because they averaged over 40% support from women, gasoline prices increase and high inflation. The supreme court ruling on abortion at the end of June had a negative effect on republicans women support as they fell below 40%.


Democrats took their first lead in July 18th by 0.5% and continue to lead. In August 23rd,2022, Democrats won the special election for the New York 19th district that Biden had won 1.6% in 2020 vs Trump and a district repulicans were meant to win to flip the house. This seat was rated Toss Up to Lean R and polling indicated a big Republican win. The win showed the environment was changing in Democrats favor with democratic voters energized since the Dobbs ruling and polls have also followed.

Women voters are important and more important to Democrats winning, they will need a double digit edge against the Republicans in November to win.
